Network/Graph Theory uses in Weblink analysis

With the recent explosion of publicly available high detailed biological data, the analysis of molecular networks has gained significant interest. Its use with regards to disease cannot be overstated. One can track the outbreak of disease from village to village, country to country. This has led to the development of network medicine. Recent examples of application of network theory in biology include understanding Cell Cycle. The interactions between physiological systems like brain, heart,and eyes can be regarded as a physiological network.
